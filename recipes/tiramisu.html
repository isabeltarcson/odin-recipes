Tiramisu

Coffee soaked lady fingers, sweet mascarpone, and cocoa powder ontop make for a delicious and easy Tiramisu! A no-bake desert that can be made in advance. This tiramisu recipe does not use raw eggs, substituting whipped cream instead.

Ingredients you'll need:

  • Ladyfingers bought or homemade
  • Mascarpone or cream cheese, but it wouldn't be the same
  • Coffee or espresso
  • Heavy Whipped cream
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Cocoa Powder for dusting ontop

Steps

  1. In a medium sized bowl, beat the mascarpone, cream, sugar, and vanilla together until stiff peaks form
  2. In a shallow bowl, dip the ladyfingers in the coffee or espresso. Just a short dip, not a soak!
  3. Smoothly layer the mascarpone/whipped cream mixture on top of the ladyfingers
  4. Repeat, add another layer of ladyfingers and mascarpone/whipped cream mixture.
  5. Dust cocoa powder ontop

Keep stored in the refridgerator, tiramisu will keep for 2 to 3 days!
